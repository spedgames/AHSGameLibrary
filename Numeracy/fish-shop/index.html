How to Play

  1. Browse the fish shop on the right — each fish has a price tag.

  3. Use the money buttons to select the exact amount.
  4. Press Pay and watch your fish swim into the aquarium!
  5. Buy 5 fish to fill your aquarium and win! 🎉
