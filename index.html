Welcome to AHS Game Library!

AHS Game Library is a collection of educational games and interactive applications specially designed for students with autism.

The games and applications in this library were created by teachers from Rainbow Centre Admiral Hill School to support learning across four key domains: Literacy, Numeracy, Social Emotional Learning, and Daily Living. Each activity is thoughtfully designed to provide engaging, meaningful, and accessible learning experiences for students of different abilities.

Our goal is to make games more accessible for students with autism — not only as a learning tool, but also as a source of enjoyment, confidence, and independence. Through interactive and motivating activities, we hope to create opportunities for every student to learn, explore, and succeed at their own pace.

We also hope to support families by providing simple and meaningful resources that parents and caregivers can easily use at home to practise and reinforce skills together.

Above all, AHS Game Library is created with the belief that learning should be accessible, encouraging, and enjoyable for every child.
